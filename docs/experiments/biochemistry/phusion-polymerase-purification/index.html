    Purification of Phusion Polymerase v1.0.0¶

    Setup¶

    Resuspension buffer¶

    Final Vol / Wt Stock / M.W.
    dH2O up to 20 ml Dissolve rest in 10ml first before bringing up to 20ml
    50mM Tris pH 8 1 ml 1M
    50mM dextrose 1 ml / 180mg 1M / 180 M.W.
    1mM EDTA 40 µl 0.5M
    1mM PMSF 200 µl 100mM or 17.4mg in 1 ml 100% EtOH / 174.2 M.W.
    5mM β-mercaptoethanol 7 µl 14.2M BioRad

    Lysis, Low-salt (A), High-salt (B) buffers¶

    • Make 1L of 2X buffer without salt:
    2X Vol / Wt Stock / M.W.
    10mM Tris pH 8 10 ml 1M
    1mM EDTA 2 ml 0.5M
    0.5mM PMSF 5 ml 100 mM or 87 mg in 5 ml 100% EtOH / 174.2 M. W.
    0.5 % Tween 20 5 ml 100 %
    0.5 % Igepal CA630 / NP40 (not Nonidet40 ) 5 ml 100 %
    5mM β-mercaptoethanol 390 µl 14.2M
    H2O to 500 ml -
    • To make 100 ml of lysis buffer with 50mM KCl, add 1.65 ml of 3M KCl to 50 ml of 2X buffer and bring up to 100 ml.
    • To make 400 ml of low salt buffer (A) with 100mM KCl, add 13.2 ml of 3M KCl to 200 ml of 2X buffer and bring up to 400 ml.
    • To make 400 ml of high salt buffer (B) with 500mM KCl, add 65.8 ml of 3M KCl to 200 ml of 2X buffer and bring up to 400 ml.
    • Keep all buffers on ice.

    Storage buffer¶

    2X Vol / Wt Stock / M.W.
    20mM Tris pH 8 2 ml 1M
    100 mM KCl 3.6 ml 3M
    0.1mM EDTA 20 µl 0.5M
    0.5 % Tween 20 500 µl 100 %
    0.5 % Igepal CA630 / NP40 (not Nonidet40 ) 500 µl 100 %
    1mM DTT 100 µl 1M
    H2O to 50 ml -
    • Add equal volume of 100% glycerol after concentrating the protein (see below).

    Procedure¶

    • Streak strain 238.2 from Rasi's postdoc box on LB + Chl 25 + Kan 50 plates overnight at 37C.
    • Next day, start overnights from all the plated cells in 20ml of SuperBroth (see recipe here) with 50 µg/ml Kan and 25 µg / ml Chl.
    • Dilute overnights to a OD600 of ~0.1 in the morning in 1L of SuperBroth (3L flask) and grow with shaking at 250rpm, 37C.
    • Check OD after a few hours and when it hits 0.6, add 1mM IPTG (1ml of 1M stock).
    • Grow for 5 more hours.
    • Harvest by centrifuging 500ml/1L bottles, 10,000rpm, 4C, 5 min.
    • Wash twice with 100ml of ice cold 1X PBS.
    • Resuspend pellet in 20ml of re-suspension buffer in a 50ml tube.
    • Freeze in LN2 and store at -80C until ready to process.
    • Thaw cells, add 20 ml of same resuspension buffer as above containing 1mg/ml lysozyme. Split 20 ml into 2×50 ml Falcon tubes.
    • Rotate or shake well at room temperature until cells have lysed.
    • Sonicate 8 times × 1 min on ice water with 1 min interval in between. Max speed Microtip.
    • Add 20 ml of lysis buffer to each of the 20 ml aliquots.
    • Heat at 75C for 60 min.
    • Spin at 20,000g for 20 min at 4C in the Strong lab centrifuge.
    • Transfer supernatant to a fresh tube.
    • Measure supernatant volume and add 3M KCl to a final concentration of 100 mM.
    • Store 50µl of lysate for SDS-Page analysis.
    • Filter through a 0.45 µM filter.
    • Equilibrate 5ml cation exchange column (GE HiTRAP CM-FF, first try HiTRAP SP column in Strong lab) with 2 CV water → 2 CV buffer A → 2 CV buffer B → 2 CV buffer A.
    • Load sample.
    • Wash with 4 CV buffer A.
    • Elute with buffer A → B gradient.
    • Run SDS-PAGE of every other fraction near the peak along with the original lysate.
    • Pool fractions that contain the protein.
    • Concentrate to 1ml using a 30kD-cutoff 15ml Centrifugal concentrator.
    • Fill with 2X storage buffer and concentrate again to 1ml.
    • Take 2×500µl aliquots and add equal volume of glycerol before storing 50µl aliquots at -20C.
